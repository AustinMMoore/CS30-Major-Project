Your goal is to defeat as many monsters as you can before they defeat you. Defeating monsters gains you gold, representing your score for the game.
Each turn you draw a hand of four cards from your deck, and playing cards discards them. When you run out of cards from your deck, your discard shuffles into your empty deck.
